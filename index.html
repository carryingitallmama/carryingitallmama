Quinn Ouellette

"This didn't come from a place of strength. It came from survival — from the sleepless nights, the exhaustion that sleep doesn't fix, and the quiet of asking why no one says this out loud."

25 weeks pregnant, mama to a toddler, and carrying it all in real time. Quinn started this podcast because those conversations needed to happen — the ones about what motherhood actually feels like when no one is watching.
